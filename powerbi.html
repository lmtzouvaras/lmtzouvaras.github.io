Business Intelligence and Interactive Visualisations

Quality Assurance Report - Interactive Dashboard

An interactive dashboard for a Quality Assurance Report. The viewer can interact with a dashboard displaying statistics and comparisons concerning the quality of the samples taken for QA. Information can be filtered for specific supervisors or employees under them. There are two views, an overall quality tab for the whole company and an employee performance tab, showing personal statistics per employee and comparisons with company average. Color coded KPI Gauges have also been created for a quick and easy evaluation of employee performance.

Top Youtube Channels and User Engagement Analysis

An interactive dashboard showcasing the top global Youtube Channels based on subscriptions, uploads and total views. Along with the visualisation of the aforementioned, metrics on user engagement have been estimated and are displayed as KPIs to facilitate estimates on potential marketing investments in these channels.
